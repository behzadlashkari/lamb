About us

LAMB Design is an interior design company run by Lisa Sharistani. We are based in North London and provide a full range of services from bespoke interiors and furniture design to lighting and electrical plans for projects of all sizes.

Lisa manages sthe business, drawing on her many years of practical experience in fashion & marketing including working as a stylist for Elle and as a fashion buyer for an international retailer.

She has a BSc in International Fashion Marketing and is a graduate of KLC with a Certificate in Interior Design. Her work focuses on technical hand drawing, lighting design, colour theory & spatial awareness and she has a keen interest in the history of style.
