Project name

The design for this large four bedroom period property, set over three floors in a residential area of North London involved a complete refurbishment including new structures, walls, windows, electrics and plumbing. Furnishing & finishes to all rooms were updated, the bathroom was remodeled with marble, concrete and brushed chrome and a compact cloakroom and utility room in micro concrete were installed within the basement. A neutral pallet was employed in this family home of grey, blush and brass, with feature lighting and fireplaces re installed to add charm to the living spaces.

A mix of minimal and contemporary style furniture was selected to contrast against the period features and fun was taken within the toddlers room taking a modern approach of monochrome and Scandi design. Key design features include the use of bespoke joinery to provide storage space under the basement staircase, bespoke concrete furniture & re installing original features within the plasterwork, window shutters and staircase.
